About the Project


This series of webpages was created to explain how the internet was created as well as how it works at a beginner level. Topics such as the creation and regulation of the internet as well as domain name addresses, IP addresses, HTTP protocol and more will be discussed on future pages. With this, we hope to enlighten other less tech-savvy individuals on these topics, as to provide a better understanding on how to stay safe on the internet. The first step in staying safe is knowledge. With this background knowledge about the internet, we hope our readers will stay safe.
