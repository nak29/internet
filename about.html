About the Project


This series of webpages was created to explain how the internet works at a beginner level. Topics such as domain name addresses, IP addresses, HTTP protocol and more will be discussed onn future pages. With this, we hope to enlighten other less tech-savvy individuals on these topics, as to provide a better understanding on how to stay safe on the internet. The first step in staying safe is knowledge.
